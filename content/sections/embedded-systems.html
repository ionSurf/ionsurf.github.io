Redefining how we connect.

The Internet of things (IoT) is the network of physical devices, vehicles, home appliances, and other items embedded with electronics, software, sensors, actuators, and connectivity which enables these things to connect, collect and exchange data. It's taking over the way we experience the world today and will keep tying bonds between the physical and the virtual realm. I've been delving this industry for the past 6 years while building commercial and artistic products. My fascination for it comes from watching th world turning a little bit more cyborg every second.

Solar Powered Surveillance Station

An AWS GreenGrass core mounted on a DragonBoard 410C with ambient sensors that report to an AWS Lambda instance in the cloud, saving data into a DynamoDB database. A face-recognizing system with a camera is setup, triggering alerts for people not recognized in the area. It’s solar powered for portability purposes.

iButler - Home Automation Through Facebook

A home automation system is implemented using a GreenGrass Core, AWS IoT devices, a Lambda instance and a conversational interface. Through questions, a user can ask what’s the state of the lights connected to the GreenGrass core as well as turn them on and off. A practical example of service and product automation.
